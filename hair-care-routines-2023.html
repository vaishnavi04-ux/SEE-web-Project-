Hair Care Routines for All Types

Healthy hair starts with a consistent routine tailored to your hair type. Here are expert tips and product recommendations to maintain vibrant, nourished hair every day.

Haircare Precautions & Tips

  • Use sulfate-free shampoo suitable for your hair type.
  • Condition regularly and don't skip deep conditioning once a week.
  • Avoid excessive heat styling and use heat protectants when needed.
  • Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends.
  • Maintain hydration and eat a balanced diet rich in vitamins.
